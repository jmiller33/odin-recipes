Simplified Southern Style Chicken and Dumplings

Description

This recipe is for one of the first things I ever learned to cook, and my family's most famous dish in our community, Southern Style Chicken and Dumplings. It's a great warm comfort food, that is perfect at any time of the year! It pairs really well with fried chicken, or can be enjoyed on its own! It can also be made extremely easily, and in large quanities! Great for family get togethers or potlucks! It can be made in a more complicated way, but this is the simples method, that even someone who never cooks can follow!


Ingredients

  • 1 can of pre made biscuit dough
  • flour
  • chicken broth
  • salt (1/2 tsp)
  • pepper (1/2 tsp)

Steps

  1. Add the chicken broth to a large stock pot or soup pot, then bring to a low boil.
  2. Coat the biscuits in flour, flatten, and tear into small pieces. Add the pieces to the boiling broth, stirring occasionally.
  3. Allow the biscuit pieces to cook until they swell and become tender, and the broth thickens.
  4. Remove from heat, serve, and enjoy! It's that easy!
